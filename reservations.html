Smith’s Luau on Kauai is one of the island’s top Hawaiian luaus. What makes it special is its setting in a torch-lit lagoon amphitheater along the banks of the Wailua River and surrounded by tropical gardens. Enjoy a variety of cultural presentations, which are followed by a delicious luau buffet dinner that includes island favorites, such as kalua pork, chicken adobo, teriyaki beef, mahi mahi (island fish), lomi salmon and a variety of salads, vegetables, rice and of course, dessert.

Dinner is followed by an exciting show featuring music and dances from Hawaii, Samoa, Tahits, New Zealand, the Philippines and Japan. Smith's Tropical Luau has it all!

  • Shell lei greeting
  • Enjoy a stroll through the 30-acre tropical gardens or take a narrated tram tour
  • Traditional imu ceremony – learn about the ancient Hawaiian cooking method where kalua pig is wrapped in ti leaves and cooked in an earthen oven
  • Delicious luau dinner featuring a wide variety of delicacies
  • Spectacular Rhythms of Aloha luau show

special instructions

  • Where and When:

    Check in 30 minutes prior to your requested tour time at the Kauai Backcountry Adventures office, located at 3-4131 Kuhio Highway, Lihue, HI 96766. View Map Look for the wooden fence and a sign that says "Kauai Backcountry Adventures."

  • Restrictions:

    Participants must be 5 years and older AND 43" (110 cm) or taller. Weight limit is 300 pounds (136 kg) Children 15 years and under must be accompanied by an adult 18 years and older. Those 16-17 years old may attend the tour without an adult, however, a parent must come in to check in the minor and sign a waiver. Not recommended for anyone claustrophobic or those with back, hip or knee problems. Women up to the second trimester of pregnancy may participate with written physician approval.

  • Special Notes:

    You can rent a wetsuit for $10 or buy one for $30. Unless extreme weather prohibits, tours go out rain or shine.

  • Operation Days:

    Daily

  • Duration:

    Approximately 3 hours

  • Vendor:

    Kauai Backcountry Adventures

  • Island:

    Kauai

  • Capacity:

    20

  • Equipment:

    Tube, gloves and headlights provided.
